Before you scan

Follow these tips for the best result:

☀️ Use natural light — sit facing a window. Turn off your flash and overhead lights.
📐 Angle slightly — tilt your phone 10–15° off-center to avoid corneal reflection.
📏 Hold at arm's length — about 30cm away. Too close causes blur and glare.
👁 Keep your eye open — look slightly up and to the side of the camera.
📱 On mobile — gently pull down your lower eyelid to expose the inner pink tissue. This gives the clearest nutrient signals.

Medical Disclaimer

Vytreos is a wellness exploration tool, not a medical device. Results are generated by AI based on visual patterns in photographs and are for general wellness awareness only.

Vytreos does not:

  • Diagnose any medical condition, disease, or deficiency
  • Replace professional medical advice, examination, or treatment
  • Provide clinically validated or FDA-cleared results
  • Substitute for laboratory blood tests or formal eye exams

Nutrient levels shown are AI-estimated wellness indicators only. Actual nutrient status can only be confirmed through clinical testing by a qualified healthcare provider.

If you have health concerns, please consult a licensed physician, ophthalmologist, or registered dietitian.
